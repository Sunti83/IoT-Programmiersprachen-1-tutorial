My Travel Blog

Why I travel

Travelling is my passion! I love exploring new places, getting to know new people, learn about their way of life, their culture and language. And of course I love to try new delicious food! Come with me and explore with me, see new points of view, enjoy landscapes, nature shots and so much more!

London

I visited my ex-roomate in London where we spent time at Hydepark, took pictures in front of Buckingham Palace, Big Ben and Tower Bridge. Then we devoured a lovely meal at the Mandarin Oriental Hotel and had drinks at a bar next to St. Paul's Cathedral. It was short but amazing!

Period of visit

From 5th to 6th June 2015

Seoul

I was fortunate to have a 12 hrs stopover in Seoul, South Korea and was offered the free transit tour at the airport. I visited the summer palace Changdeokung. I loved the mix of old and new buildings in the city and enjoyed a really tasty lunch at a little restaurant in a tiny alley. I would like to travel there again and stay longer.

Period of visit

From 4th to 5th December 2016

Canada

I used to live in Canada and visited many places. In the picture you can see Medicine Lake, close to Jasper, Alberta. The landscape and wildlife are beautiful and still amaze me everytime.
